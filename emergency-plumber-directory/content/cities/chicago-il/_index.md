## What to Look for in an Emergency Plumber

### 1. Genuine 24/7 Availability
Not every company that advertises "emergency service" actually answers the phone at 3 a.m. on a Sunday. Look for plumbers who clearly state their hours and have a live dispatcher — not just a voicemail box — so you get real help when you need it.

### 2. Licensed and Insured in Illinois
Always verify that your plumber holds a valid Illinois plumbing license and carries both liability insurance and workers' compensation. This protects you if something goes wrong during the repair. Reputable companies like those listed here make licensing information readily available.

### 3. Transparent, Upfront Pricing
Emergency plumbing naturally costs more than a scheduled appointment, but that doesn't mean you should be surprised by the bill. Look for companies that offer clear pricing over the phone or provide a written estimate before work begins — even in urgent situations.

### 4. Local Experience and Strong Reviews
A plumber who has worked extensively in Chicago understands the city's building codes, permit requirements, and common plumbing quirks. Check Google and Yelp reviews for consistent patterns of punctuality, professionalism, and quality workmanship — not just a handful of five-star ratings.

## Questions to Ask Before Hiring

- **What is your guaranteed response time?** In a plumbing emergency, every minute counts. Ask specifically how long it will take for a technician to arrive at your address.
- **Are your plumbers licensed and insured?** Always ask directly — a trustworthy company will answer without hesitation and can provide documentation.
- **How do you handle pricing for after-hours calls?** Understand any overtime fees, diagnostic charges, or service call minimums before agreeing to anything.
- **Do you pull permits for major repairs?** For significant work like sewer line replacement or water heater installation, permits are legally required in Chicago. A reputable plumber handles this for you automatically.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much does an emergency plumber cost in Chicago?**
Emergency plumbing rates in Chicago typically range from $150 to $400+ for after-hours service calls, depending on the time, nature of the problem, and parts required. Always request an estimate before work begins.

**How quickly can an emergency plumber reach me in Chicago?**
Most reputable emergency plumbers in Chicago aim for a response time of 30 to 90 minutes within the city limits, though this can vary during severe weather or peak demand periods.

**What qualifies as a plumbing emergency?**
Burst or frozen pipes, sewage backups, no hot water in winter, gas line concerns, and major leaks that can't be controlled by shutting off the water supply all qualify as plumbing emergencies requiring immediate professional attention.
