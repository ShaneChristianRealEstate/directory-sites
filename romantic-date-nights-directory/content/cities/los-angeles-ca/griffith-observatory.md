## Griffith Observatory — Los Angeles, CA

⭐ 4.7 stars (17,910 reviews) · 2800 E Observatory Rd, Los Angeles, CA 90027

Perched high in the Santa Monica Mountains above the glittering sprawl of Los Angeles, Griffith Observatory offers couples something genuinely rare in a city this vast — a shared sense of wonder. As the sun dips behind the Pacific horizon and the city lights begin their nightly performance below, the hilltop setting transforms into one of the most naturally romantic backdrops in Southern California. The air is cooler up here, the noise of the city softens, and suddenly the universe feels both enormous and intimate.

Inside, the observatory's Art Deco halls invite you to wander together through exhibits exploring space, time, and the cosmos — conversations that have a way of turning surprisingly deep. Step outside onto the rooftop terrace and the panoramic view stretches from downtown's glowing towers to the distant shimmer of the Pacific. Whether you're tracing constellations overhead or simply watching the city pulse beneath you, there's a quiet magic here that makes even a casual evening feel cinematic.

### What to Expect

- **Sweeping city panoramas** from the open-air terrace, especially stunning at sunset and after dark
- **Telescopes available** for public stargazing on clear evenings
- **Immersive planetarium shows** inside the Samuel Oschin Planetarium
- **Iconic architecture** and beautifully lit grounds ideal for an evening stroll
- **Free general admission** to the building and grounds
