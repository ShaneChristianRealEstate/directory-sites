## The Getty | Los Angeles, CA

*1200 Getty Center Dr, Los Angeles, CA 90049 · ⭐ 4.8 (36,602 reviews)*

---

Perched high in the Santa Monica Mountains, The Getty offers couples something genuinely rare in a city this sprawling: a shared sense of wonder. The journey begins before you even step inside — a tram ride up the hillside delivers you into a world of travertine pavilions, cascading gardens, and skies that seem impossibly close. There's a natural rhythm to the place that invites slow, unhurried conversation.

Inside, world-class European paintings, sculptures, and decorative arts give couples something meaningful to discover together, while the outdoor spaces reward those who simply want to wander. The Central Garden — a living artwork in itself — shifts with the seasons, and the panoramic views stretching from downtown Los Angeles to the Pacific have a way of making every visit feel cinematic. As the afternoon light softens, it becomes one of the most quietly romantic settings in all of Southern California.

### What to Expect

- Stunning 360-degree views of the LA Basin and coastline
- Rotating exhibitions alongside a celebrated permanent collection
- Beautifully landscaped gardens ideal for a leisurely stroll
- An on-site café and restaurant with terrace seating
- Free museum admission (parking fee applies)
