## Little Island — Pier 55, Hudson River Park, New York, NY

*★ 4.7 stars — 17,488 reviews*

Rising like a dream from the Hudson River, Little Island is one of New York City's most enchanting outdoor escapes — a floating park perched on tulip-shaped concrete pillars that feels entirely removed from the city's relentless energy. Strolling its winding paths together, couples discover sweeping views of the Manhattan skyline, the New Jersey Palisades, and the glittering river below, all wrapped in lush plantings that shift beautifully with the seasons.

Whether you arrive at golden hour to watch the sun melt behind the water or linger into the evening when the park softens into quiet intimacy, Little Island rewards those who slow down. The undulating topography — gentle hills, hidden nooks, and open-air amphitheater spaces — makes every visit feel like a gentle adventure, somewhere between a countryside stroll and a rooftop rendezvous, right in the heart of the West Village.
