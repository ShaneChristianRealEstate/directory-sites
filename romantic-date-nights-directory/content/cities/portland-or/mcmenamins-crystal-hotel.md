## A Soulful Stay in the Heart of Portland

Tucked into a lovingly restored historic building on SW 12th Avenue, McMenamins Crystal Hotel is the kind of place that feels like a well-kept secret between you and the city. Part of the beloved McMenamins family of quirky, art-infused properties, this boutique hotel wraps couples in an atmosphere of warm bohemian charm — exposed brick, locally commissioned artwork, and corridors that feel alive with character and story. For partners who crave something more soulful than a standard hotel room, this is Portland distilled into a single address.

The romance here lives in the details. Every corner of the Crystal Hotel invites slow mornings and long conversations, from the intimate communal spaces to the warmly lit bar downstairs. Connected to the legendary Crystal Ballroom, the energy of live music drifts through the building on weekends, giving your stay a soundtrack that feels spontaneous and alive. With a 4.5-star rating across more than 1,600 reviews, guests consistently fall for its personality-packed rooms and that rare sense of place that only truly independent properties deliver.
