## McMenamins Kennedy School · Portland, OR

*⭐ 4.6 stars · 8,857 reviews · 5736 NE 33rd Ave, Portland, OR 97211*

---

There's something undeniably romantic about stepping into a place where history and whimsy collide. McMenamins Kennedy School occupies a beautifully restored 1915 elementary school in Northeast Portland, where chalkboard hallways and converted classrooms create an atmosphere unlike anywhere else in the city. For couples who crave a date night with genuine character, this is the kind of place that sparks real conversation — the setting does half the work for you.

Wander hand-in-hand through labyrinthine corridors adorned with murals, settle into a cozy corner of the Honors Bar, or sink into a soaking pool beneath the open sky. The Kennedy School rewards slow exploration, making it ideal for couples who love discovering little moments together — a tucked-away lounge, a vintage film screening, a locally brewed pint shared in a room that once held third-grade spelling tests. It's Portland's most pleasantly disorienting date destination.

### What to Expect

- **Atmospheric bars and lounges** spread throughout the historic building, each with its own mood and décor
- **The Courtyard Restaurant** offering casual Pacific Northwest dining in a striking open-air setting
- **A soaking pool** perfect for a relaxed, intimate evening wind-down
- **Movie screenings** in the converted theater — bring your drinks
- **Hotel rooms** housed in former classrooms, ideal for a spontaneous overnight stay
