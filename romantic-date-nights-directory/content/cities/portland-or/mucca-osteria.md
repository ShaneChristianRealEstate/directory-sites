## Mucca Osteria — Portland's Romantic Italian Escape

Tucked along SW Morrison Street in the heart of Portland, Mucca Osteria wraps you in the kind of unhurried, convivial warmth that Italy does so effortlessly — and that this city's dining scene has made entirely its own. With a stellar 4.7-star rating across more than 800 reviews, it's clearly earned its place as one of Portland's most beloved neighborhood trattorias, the sort of spot where an evening quietly stretches into something memorable.

For couples, the appeal is immediate and lasting. There's an intimacy to the space that encourages lingering — over a shared pasta, a poured glass of something Italian, and the easy rhythm of unhurried conversation. The osteria's name and spirit both nod to rustic Italian tradition, and that sensibility carries through to every corner of the experience: honest, heartfelt, and deeply satisfying without ever feeling fussy or performative.
