## Oldfields–Lilly House & Gardens

Tucked within the sweeping grounds of Newfields in Indianapolis, Oldfields–Lilly House & Gardens feels like stepping into a private world built entirely for slow afternoons and lingering glances. The meticulously restored estate — once home to the prominent Lilly family — unfolds across formal gardens, sunlit meadows, and classically elegant interiors that make every visit feel like a scene from a golden-era novel. There's a genuine sense of discovery here, around every hedgerow and through every arched doorway.

For couples, the magic lies in the unhurried pace the grounds naturally inspire. Wandering hand-in-hand through the tiered gardens, pausing to admire the carriage house or the Sunken Garden's sculpted symmetry, you find yourselves naturally slowing down and actually *talking*. The estate's blend of architectural grandeur and lush, breathing greenery creates an atmosphere that's romantic without trying too hard — effortlessly beautiful in every season.

- Touring the elegantly restored early 20th-century Lilly family residence
