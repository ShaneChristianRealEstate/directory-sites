## St. Elmo Steak House — Indianapolis, IN

*127 S Illinois St, Indianapolis, IN 46225 · ⭐ 4.6 (6,888 reviews)*

Few restaurants in the Midwest carry the weight of history and romance quite like St. Elmo Steak House. Open since 1902, this legendary Indianapolis institution wraps you in the warmth of dark wood, white tablecloths, and the kind of hushed, expectant energy that makes an ordinary evening feel genuinely momentous. The moment you step through the door, the city outside fades — and something more intimate takes over.

For couples, St. Elmo offers something rarer than a good meal: a sense of occasion. Whether you're celebrating a milestone anniversary or simply want a night that feels worthy of dressing up, the attentive service and storied atmosphere conspire to make you feel like the only two people in the room. The legendary shrimp cocktail alone — famously bold and unapologetically spicy — has a way of breaking the ice and sparking conversation all on its own.
