## Franklin Park Conservatory and Botanical Gardens

Step through the doors of Franklin Park Conservatory and Botanical Gardens and feel the rest of the world quietly fall away. A stunning Victorian-era glass pavilion anchors this beloved Columbus landmark, where lush tropical palms brush the ceiling and the air carries the warm, green scent of something perpetually in bloom. For couples, there's an almost effortless romance in wandering from one biome to the next — from the humid warmth of the Pacific Island Water Garden to the cool drama of the Himalayan Mountain Environment — each space offering its own intimate atmosphere.

Beyond the living plant collections, the conservatory regularly hosts seasonal exhibitions, special evening events, and stunning Dale Chihuly glass installations that scatter jewel-toned light across the gardens like something from a dream. Whether you're strolling hand-in-hand past orchids and bromeliads or pausing to admire a sculpture nestled among the foliage, the experience feels unhurried and genuinely transportive — a rare quality in any city setting.
