## Le Méridien Columbus, The Joseph

Tucked into the vibrant heart of Columbus's Short North Arts District, Le Méridien Columbus, The Joseph is the kind of place that transforms an ordinary evening into something worth remembering. The hotel's art-forward identity — anchored by an impressive rotating collection of contemporary works — gives every corridor, lounge, and lobby a sense of discovery, as though the building itself is curated for curious, creative souls. For couples, that shared sense of wonder is a romantic catalyst all its own.

With a stellar 4.5-star rating across nearly 800 reviews, this is a property that consistently delivers on its promise of sophistication without stuffiness. Whether you're sipping cocktails in an elegantly designed bar, retreating to a beautifully appointed room after a night out on High Street, or simply soaking in the buzz of one of Columbus's most dynamic neighborhoods, The Joseph keeps the atmosphere effortlessly intimate. It's the rare hotel that makes you feel like insiders, even on your first visit.
