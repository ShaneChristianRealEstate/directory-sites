## W Minneapolis – The Foshay

*821 S Marquette Ave, Minneapolis, MN 55402 · ⭐ 4.3 (2,586 reviews)*

Perched inside the landmark Foshay Tower — a 1929 Art Deco icon that once stood as the tallest building in the American Midwest — the W Minneapolis invites couples into a world where storied history meets bold, contemporary energy. The moment you step into the lobby, the dramatic interplay of original architectural details and the W's signature moody, design-forward aesthetic sets an unmistakably romantic tone. This is downtown Minneapolis at its most cinematic.

Whether you're lingering over cocktails at the bar, retreating to a sleek, beautifully appointed room, or simply soaking in the skyline views that made this tower legendary, the Foshay delivers the kind of effortless glamour that turns an ordinary evening into a memory. The buzzing downtown location puts you within easy reach of Minneapolis's best dining, the Orpheum Theatre, and the city's vibrant nightlife — making it an ideal anchor for a full romantic night out.
