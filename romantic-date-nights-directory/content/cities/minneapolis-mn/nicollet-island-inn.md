## A Romantic Retreat on the Mississippi

Tucked away on a peaceful island in the middle of the Mississippi River, Nicollet Island Inn offers couples something increasingly rare in the heart of Minneapolis — genuine seclusion without sacrificing city convenience. The 1893 historic building exudes old-world charm, with exposed brick, warm wood details, and a sense of timeless elegance that immediately sets the mood for romance. Crossing onto the island feels like stepping out of ordinary life and into something a little more intentional.

The inn's intimate scale means you're never lost in a crowd, and the surrounding parkland creates a natural sense of privacy that larger downtown hotels simply can't replicate. Whether you're lingering over a candlelit dinner in the restored dining room or stealing quiet moments along the riverbank at dusk, there's a gentle, unhurried quality to an evening here that invites couples to slow down and actually connect.
