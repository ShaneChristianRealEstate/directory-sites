## Minneapolis Sculpture Garden · Minneapolis, MN

⭐ 4.7 stars (7,616 reviews) · 725 Vineland Pl, Minneapolis, MN 55403

---

Strolling hand-in-hand through an open-air gallery, where art and nature intertwine across eleven beautifully landscaped acres — that's the quiet magic of the Minneapolis Sculpture Garden. Operated in partnership with the Walker Art Center, this beloved landmark offers couples a rare combination of cultural richness and unhurried outdoor wandering. Whether you're pausing in front of a striking contemporary piece or finding a quiet bench to share a moment, the garden moves at whatever pace feels right for the two of you.

Few backdrops in the Twin Cities are as effortlessly romantic as this one. The iconic *Spoonbridge and Cherry* fountain anchors the experience with a sense of playful wonder, and the surrounding greenery shifts beautifully with the seasons — lush and golden in autumn, dreamily snow-dusted in winter. With the Minneapolis skyline framing the horizon, every glance offers a picture-perfect moment worth savoring together.

### What to Expect

- Leisurely strolls through curated contemporary sculpture collections in a park-like setting
- The famous *Spoonbridge and Cherry* — an iconic and genuinely photogenic centerpiece
- Seasonal beauty that transforms the experience year-round
- Easy access to the Walker Art Center for couples who want to extend their cultural adventure
- A free, welcoming atmosphere with no reservations required
