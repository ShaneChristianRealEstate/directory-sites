## Hotel EMC2, Autograph Collection — Chicago, IL

Nestled in Chicago's vibrant Streeterville neighborhood, Hotel EMC2 is where art meets intellect in the most romantically unexpected way. The hotel's science-and-art philosophy — a nod to Einstein's famous equation — creates an atmosphere that feels genuinely unlike anywhere else in the city. Couples who appreciate beauty with a brain will find themselves captivated by the curated artwork, the warm lighting that softens every corner, and a sense of inspired curiosity that makes even a quiet evening in feel like an adventure.

Beyond the aesthetic, this Autograph Collection property delivers the kind of attentive, unhurried hospitality that transforms a simple overnight stay into a genuine escape. Just steps from the Magnificent Mile and Lake Michigan, you're perfectly positioned to explore one of Chicago's most energetic districts before retreating to a sanctuary that rewards slowing down. With a 4.6-star rating earned across over 1,500 reviews, the consistency here speaks for itself.
