Perched at the northern edge of the Magnificent Mile where Lake Shore Drive meets the shimmering expanse of Lake Michigan, The Drake is one of Chicago's most storied and romantic escapes. Since its doors first opened, this landmark hotel has carried an air of old-world elegance — marble lobbies, gilded ceilings, and the kind of hushed grandeur that makes every visit feel like a special occasion. For couples, simply walking through the entrance together feels like stepping into a golden era of romance.

The atmosphere here rewards those who slow down and linger. Whether you're settling into the legendary **Palm Court** for afternoon tea, sharing cocktails in the intimate **Coq d'Or lounge**, or gazing out at the lake from a lakefront room as city lights begin to shimmer, The Drake offers layers of romance that unfold naturally. It's the kind of place where conversations deepen and moments become memories.
