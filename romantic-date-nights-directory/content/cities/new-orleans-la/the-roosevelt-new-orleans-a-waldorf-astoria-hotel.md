## A Grand Romance in the Heart of New Orleans

Few hotels in the American South carry the weight of legend quite like The Roosevelt New Orleans. Stepping through its doors on Roosevelt Way feels like crossing into another era — one of jazz-soaked evenings, gilded corridors, and the kind of unhurried elegance that invites couples to slow down and truly be present with one another. With a stellar 4.6-star rating across more than 6,000 guest reviews, this Waldorf Astoria jewel has earned its place as one of the city's most celebrated romantic retreats.

The hotel's storied atmosphere does much of the courting for you. Whether you're sharing cocktails in the legendary Sazerac Bar — birthplace of one of America's most iconic cocktails — or drifting through the breathtaking lobby adorned with its famous holiday grandeur, every corner feels curated for connection. New Orleans already hums with romance, and The Roosevelt amplifies that magic tenfold, placing you steps from the French Quarter while wrapping you in a sanctuary of refined luxury.

### What to Expect

- **Iconic cocktail culture** at the historic Sazerac Bar, steeped in New Orleans tradition
- **Beautifully appointed rooms** that blend timeless Waldorf Astoria elegance with Southern warmth
- **Prime location** within easy reach of the French Quarter, live music, and fine dining
- **Attentive, world-class service** that makes special occasions feel genuinely extraordinary
- **A grand, atmospheric lobby** perfect for that unforgettable arrival moment together

**Perfect For:** Anniversary celebrations, milestone birthdays, romantic weekend getaways, and special occasion splurges.
