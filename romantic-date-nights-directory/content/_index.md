## How to Plan the Perfect Date Night

**1. Start with a vibe, not a venue.**
Before you book, ask yourselves: Do you want lively and festive, or quiet and intimate? Knowing the *mood* you're chasing helps you choose the right romantic restaurant, neighborhood, and experience.

**2. Reserve early, especially for special occasions.**
The best anniversary dinner spots and sought-after date night experiences fill up fast — particularly on weekends and holidays. Booking ahead also gives you time to arrange any personal touches, like a special dessert or a room upgrade.

**3. Layer your evening.**
The most memorable nights have a natural flow — drinks at a rooftop bar, dinner somewhere extraordinary, then a stroll through a lit-up city street. Use our city guides to build a full evening, not just a single reservation.
