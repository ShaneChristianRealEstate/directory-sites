## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does water damage restoration take in Las Vegas?**
The timeline depends on the severity of the damage. Minor incidents may be resolved in two to three days, while significant flooding or structural damage can take one to two weeks or longer. Your restoration company should provide a realistic project timeline after an initial assessment.

**Does homeowner's insurance cover water damage in Nevada?**
Most standard homeowner's policies cover sudden and accidental water damage (like a burst pipe), but not flooding from external sources or damage caused by neglect. Always review your policy and consult your insurer — a good restoration company can help you understand what's likely covered.

**Is mold always a concern after water damage?**
In Las Vegas's dry climate, mold risk is lower than in humid regions — but it's never zero. If moisture isn't fully extracted and dried within 24 to 48 hours, mold growth is possible, especially in hidden spaces like wall cavities and under flooring. Professional drying equipment and moisture monitoring are essential.
